EPICENTR K, a leading Ukrainian DIY retail chain founded on December 6, 2003, offers top-quality home improvement goods, building materials, and gardening products. As a “one-stop store” we provide excellent products and services. EPICENTR K joined the European DIY Retail Association (EDRA) in 2010.
